About Us

Cyberland are a family owned and managed Theme Park in the South East of England. Over the years we have grown from a small park with only 12 employees to a nationally recognised place to spend the day attracting over 230,000 visitors every year!

We have a reputation for delivering quality entertainment in a safe environment for all ages. We sincerely believe in our motto “Where the fun never stops!”

You may use the Cyberland map to learn more about all of the activities available at our amusement park.

Our Premier Attractions

Imagine stepping into a world where gravity bends, and ordinary rules no longer apply. Our 40+ rides and attractions cater to every age group. From the iconic Corkscrew, Britain’s first double-loop rollercoaster, to the enchanting Pirate Ship, there’s something for everyone.
